Overview

Assassin’s Creed is an action-adventure game series set across different historical periods. Players take on the role of Assassins fighting against the Templars — two ancient secret societies with conflicting ideologies. The story blends stealth, parkour, and combat with historical fiction and sci-fi elements through the use of the Animus, a device that lets modern characters relive ancestral memories.

Key Info

  • Developer: Ubisoft
  • First Released: 2007
  • Latest Main Title: Assassin's Creed Mirage (2023)
  • Genre: Action-Adventure / Stealth
  • Platforms: PC, PlayStation, Xbox

Cast & Creators

The series was created by Patrice Désilets, Jade Raymond, and Corey May. Over the years, voice actors like Nolan North (Desmond Miles), Roger Craig Smith (Ezio Auditore), and Abubakar Salim (Bayek) have brought iconic characters to life. Ubisoft Montreal and several other Ubisoft studios have developed the games.

Main Characters

  • Altair Ibn-La'Ahad – The original Assassin
  • Ezio Auditore da Firenze – The legendary Renaissance assassin
  • Bayek of Siwa – Founder of the Brotherhood
  • Kassandra / Alexios – From ancient Greece
  • Eivor – The Viking warrior

Reviews

Assassin's Creed games are generally praised for their immersive historical settings and storytelling. While some entries received criticism for repetitiveness, titles like Assassin’s Creed II, Black Flag, and Origins are considered standout successes. Recent titles have leaned into RPG elements with expansive open worlds and player choice.
