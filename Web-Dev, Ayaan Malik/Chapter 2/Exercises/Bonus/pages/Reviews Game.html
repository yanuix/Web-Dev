Reviews

Assassin's Creed games are generally praised for their immersive historical settings and storytelling. While some entries received criticism for repetitiveness, titles like Assassin's Creed II, Black Flag, and Origins are considered standout successes. Recent titles have leaned into RPG elements with expansive open worlds and player choice.
